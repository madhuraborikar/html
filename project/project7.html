Chai Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons black tea leaves
  • 2-3 fresh ginger
  • 2-3 cardamom(ilaichi)

Instruction

  1. Boil 2 cup of water
  2. Add ginger, cardamom, and milk, tea leaves
  3. Boil for alomost 15-20 minutes
  4. Serves the chai to everyone
